Welcome to Therapets!

Therapets are cuddly little creatures that feed on good feelings! The happier their owners are, the healthier the Therapet is! The good feelings fuel their magic and keep them alive, and when they've had their fill and are full to the brim with magic, they give off little sparkly dust that makes others happy, too!

We're an ARPG group (Art Role Play Game) where you can create your own cuddly companion, help them grow and explore the world, decorate their personal room, and more!

Sprinkle Guide

What are Magic Sprinkles?

Magic Sprinkles are the name of the virtual currency of Therapets. You earn them by drawing anyone's Therapets, and they can be used to make changes to your own Therapet, or to buy items from the shop!

Counting Sprinkles

In order to earn Sprinkles, make a drawing, and count up how many of these it qualifies for!

Art

Headshot+1
Full Body+2
Sketchx0.5 (halve the total amount of Sprinkles, rounding up)
Lineart and Colored+1
Shading+2
Simple Background+1 (shapes and patterns, solid colors and gradients don't count)
Complex Background+3 (must have a sense of depth)
Animation+3
Pixel Art+2
Physical Crafts (plushies, sculptures, etc.)+5
Owner (if the Therapet is in the same picture as it's owner, add this. Can only count once per deviation, for one Therapet)+2
Other Therapets+1 each (in addition to all of the above, if the Therapet does not belong to you, add an extra Sprinkle!)

Writing

Every +100 words+1 sprinkle (must have at least 1 Therapet that appears through the entire story)
Other Therapets+1 each (the Therapet must not belong to you, and must contribute to the story in some way)
Owner is Present+2

Using Sprinkles

I have enough Sprinkles for a sundae, now what?

You can do two things with Sprinkles: make changes to your Therapet, and buy items from the shop.

Making Changes

First, go to the Upgrades Journal and check out what there is. Pick out what you want, and if you have enough Sprinkles, you can add it to your Therapet! Follow the rules in the journal by making a fullbody picture of your Therapet showing off its new look. In your reference sheet, make sure to link to that deviation and update your Sprinkle total. You lose Sprinkles when you Upgrade, so keep a running tally and make sure you link everything in your reference in case there's ever any problems!

The Shop

The Magic Shop sells useful and fun items, such as accessory slots and medicine. There isn't much there just yet, but there will be in the future! You can purchase some things with Sprinkles, but others cost DA points.

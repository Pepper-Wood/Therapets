Exploring

Therapets love to discover new things and learn about the world around them. They may just find something to bring back home!

There are two types of exploration: Indoor and Outdoor. They each yield different items. Some items can be used in crafting, while others can't. Try your luck!

You can explore once every Tuesday and Thursday.

Indoor Exploration

Your Therapet is exploring your home, school, store, or anywhere else that's inside! Hm, how long has it been since you last dusted?

Common Items

Finger Paint Glue String Cardboard Paper Fluff Plastic Bits Metal Wire Crayons

Common Items

Paint Bucket Fabric Plastic Chunks Electrical Wire

Rare Items

Sewing Needle Glue Gun Hammer Scissors Batteries

Outdoor Exploration

Your Therapet is ready to explore the big, wide world! Outdoor exploration requires you purchase a set of ID Tags from the Magic Shop

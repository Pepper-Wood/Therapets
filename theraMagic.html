Thera Magic

Therapets are magical creatures. They re held together with magic, it's what keeps them alive and healthy. But it's more than that, they can learn a multitude of abilities through practice!

There are six spheres of magical practice that a Therapet can learn: Mood, Nerve, Mind, Void, Zeal, and Physical. Although any Therapet can learn any (or all) of these magics, the more spread out their efforts are, the weaker individual abilities will be. If you want your Therapet to reach its fullest potential in any magic, it's best not to study more than two spheres.

Traits of the Spheres

Each Therapet has a predisposition for one or two spheres of magic. They can learn from others, but these come to them easiest. Depending on their predisposition, it can affect their personality and natural abilities

Learning Magic

To learn magic a Terapet must practice activities that promote that sphere's traits. In order to learn magic:

  • A Mentor must be present, for safety. This can be either the Therapet's Owner, or a more experienced Therapet. Therapets can't control their magic right away, they need supervision so that they don't hurt themselves or others.
  • The Therapet must have reached Magical Maturity. Lore-wise, this means they have helped their Owner find a solution to a problem that'd been plaguing them (ex. managing depression, or finding a lost item that means a lot to them). For the group, this happens when your Therapet's approved app reaches 3 weeks old.
  • You must complete a total of 3 prompts for the magic you wish to learn. The prompts below are only examples, you cannot learn magic yet.

  • Minor Telekinesis/Levitation
  • Can create illusions and help dispell hallucinations

  • Excellent memories
  • Incredible focus on tasks, doesn't let them go unfinished
  • Mind games (matching cards, focus-oriented tasks)
Zeal
  • Their biorythm can attune to their owner's, to let them know when to eat
  • Very active, love to run around and play with their owners
  • Physical activities (like tag and sports)
Physical
  • Can heal small wounds and help fade (but not cure) small scars
  • Can ease (but not cure) physical pain
  • Healing practice (focusing magic to close paper cuts)
